Who am I?

A future graduate of ISAE-SUPAERO, I'd like to put my knowledge of Data Science to work on impact projects. By "impact projects", I'm referring in particular to projects aimed at solving current environmental issues or improving the quality of life for everyone (health, education, etc.).
Having also followed Master 2 courses in Deep Learning in Singapore (NUS), I've already been able to apply my knowledge in practical cases through two internships as well as personal projects. In particular, I carried out a personal project to protect sharks (and surfers) in Australia.

If my friends had to describe me in two words, they'd use "curious" and "creative". I actually like to spend some of my free time solving everyday problems, typically at my school's Fablab when I was there. I think the constraints (material, physical, etc.) in a problem push me to be creative in finding the best solution, and that's probably why I always wanted to be an engineer.

What's the point of this website?

I sometimes carry out certain personal projects in my spare time. They're not necessarily relevant on a Resume, but they're still part of my daily life. On this site, you can find out more about the various projects I've carried out both during and outside my school career.

So enjoy your reading!

Whether you'd like to ask me a question, propose a project, or just have a chat, don't hesitate to contact me by e-mail or via LinkedIn , I'd be delighted to hear from you!
